Peter E. Murray

Peter is a technologist with a librarian background and a keen sense of how to apply the right solution to a challenge. With training in the field of Systems Analysis, he is able to translate between librarian jargon and computing/networking jargon to bring the two worlds together. He has over two decades of experience in higher education libraries — including eight years at members of the Association for Research Libraries (ARL) and ten years at library consortia — and actively participates in a wide variety of technical areas such as linked data, networking protocols, HTML/XHTML/XML markup, and metadata standards. Current interests include open source software in general and the FOLIO Project in particular, digital information privacy, and the development of open standards.
